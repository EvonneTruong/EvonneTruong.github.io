Security Cameras, Digital Video, 2018

This piece used video recordings of scurity cameras around my home. The videos are to highlight that while security camera are meant to used as a safety precaution, it still can infringe on the privacy on those living in the house.

Perspective, Digital Video, 2018

This piece explore the anxiety caused by transportation via multiple angles of common modes of mechanical transportation. The multiple videos, camera angles, and sounds combine together are meant simulate an overs stimulation state of being. The video is projection mapped into a corner of the room and is meant to isolate the viewer from the rest of the gallery.
